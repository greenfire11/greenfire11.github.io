AlQibla

The Muslim prayer time app is a beautifully designed mobile application that provides users with an easy and intuitive way to track prayer times throughout the day. With its modern, minimalist design, the app offers a streamlined user experience that allows users to quickly view daily prayer times, set reminders for upcoming prayers, and access additional information about Islamic prayer practices.The app features a clean and simple interface that is easy to navigate, with all the essential prayer time information displayed prominently on the home screen.In addition to providing users with prayer time reminders, the app also includes a range of other useful features, such as a compass that helps users to determine the direction of the qibla.

Used stack:

  • Flutter
  • Dart

MediTrack

The medicine tracking app is a sleek and user-friendly mobile application that helps users manage their medication schedules and ensure that they are taking their medications as prescribed. With its intuitive design and powerful features, the app offers users an easy and convenient way to keep track of their medications, set reminders for when to take them, and monitor their progress over time.The app's home screen provides users with an overview of their medication schedule, displaying upcoming doses and reminders in a clear and easy-to-read format. Users can also view detailed information about each medication, including dosage instructions, possible side effects, and other important information.To help users stay on top of their medication schedule, the app offers customizable reminders that can be set to go off at specific times throughout the day. These reminders can be tailored to each individual medication, allowing users to set different schedules for each medication they are taking.

Used stack:

  • Flutter
  • Dart
  • Firebase
